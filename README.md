üéØ Course Overview

This repository contains materials, codes, and coding tutorials from the course Data Science for Social Scientists, offered at Ashoka University in Spring 2025. Designed for undergraduate students in social sciences, the course introduces modern data science methods and computational tools for empirical public policy research.

The course expands students‚Äô ability to work with complex data, build reproducible workflows, create compelling visualizations, and develop research that addresses real-world governance, social development, and political science questions.

üß† Learning Objectives

By the end of the course, students were able to:

Work with structured and unstructured datasets, including spatial data.

Conduct full-scale reproducible research projects in R.

Apply machine learning, regression modelling, classification, and prediction frameworks.

Use GIS and geospatial analysis for policy and development mapping.

Use visualization for exploratory and explanatory purposes (ggplot2, maps, dashboards).

Interpret quantitative evidence to support research narratives and public policy recommendations.
